Paris, France - Complete Travel Guide

Overview:
The City of Light offers iconic landmarks, world-class museums, charming cafes, and romantic Seine river walks. A must-visit for art, history, and food lovers.

Best Time to Visit:
April-June or September-October (mild weather, fewer crowds). Avoid August (locals vacation, many shops closed).

Budget Breakdown:
- Budget: $70-100/day (hostels, street food, free museums)
- Mid-range: $150-250/day (3-star hotels, bistros, paid attractions)
- Luxury: $400+/day (5-star hotels, Michelin dining, VIP tours)

Iconic Landmarks:
- Eiffel Tower: $18-29 (summit access), book online in advance
- Louvre Museum: $18, free first Sunday of month
- Notre-Dame Cathedral: Free (currently under renovation)
- Arc de Triomphe: $13, climb for city views
- Sacré-Cœur Basilica: Free, Montmartre hilltop location

Neighborhoods to Explore:
- Le Marais: Trendy, LGBTQ+ friendly, vintage shops, Jewish quarter
- Montmartre: Artists, Sacré-Cœur, cafes, village vibe
- Latin Quarter: Students, bookshops, affordable restaurants
- Saint-Germain-des-Prés: Upscale, literary cafes, art galleries
- Canal Saint-Martin: Hipster, picnics, boutique shopping

Must-Try Food:
- Croissant from local boulangerie: $1.50-3
- Crêpes: $5-10 (sweet or savory)
- Baguette sandwich: $5-8
- Café au lait: $3-5
- Escargot: $12-18
- French onion soup: $10-15
- Macrons from Ladurée: $3 each

Hidden Gems:
- Shakespeare and Company bookstore: Iconic English bookshop
- Musée Rodin: Sculptures in beautiful garden ($13)
- Père Lachaise Cemetery: Jim Morrison, Oscar Wilde graves (free)
- Sainte-Chapelle: Stunning stained glass ($12)

Transportation:
- Metro day pass: $8
- Navigo weekly pass: $28 (unlimited travel)
- Vélib bike rental: $4/day
- River Seine cruise: $15-20
- Airport to city: RER B train $11

Day Trips:
- Versailles Palace: $20 entry, 1 hour train ($7)
- Disneyland Paris: $60-90, 45 min train
- Champagne region: $100-150 tour with tastings
- Giverny (Monet's gardens): $10 entry, 1.5 hour train

Tips:
- Buy Museum Pass ($55 for 2 days) if visiting multiple sites
- Free museum entry first Sunday each month
- Learn basic French (Bonjour, Merci, S'il vous plaît)
- Pickpockets near Eiffel Tower and metros - stay alert
- Restaurants charge for water (ask for "carafe d'eau" for free tap water)
- Tipping: Round up bill or 5-10% for excellent service
